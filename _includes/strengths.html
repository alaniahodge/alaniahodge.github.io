StrengthsQuests

Harmony

People especially talented in the Harmony theme look for consensus.
They don't enjoy conflict; rather, they seek areas of agreement.

Connectedness

People especially talented in the Connectedness theme have faith
in the links between all things. They believe there are few coincidences
and that almost every event has a reason

Includer

People especially talented in the Includer theme are accepting of others.
They show awareness of those who feel left out, and make an effort to include them.

Input

People especially talented in the Input theme have a need to collect and archive.
They may collect information, ideas, history, or even relationships.

Responsibility

People especially talented in the Responsibility theme take psychological ownership
of what they say they will do. They are committed to stable values such as honesty and loyalty
